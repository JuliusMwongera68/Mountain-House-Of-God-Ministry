Mission:To evangelize globally, train lay pastors and build sustainable faith communities tht embody Christ's teachings.

CORE VALUES

Apostolic Mindset:Emphasis on pioneering new mission fields.
Compassionate Service:Serving the physical and spiritual needs of communities.
Cultural Adaptability:Respecting cultural diversity while upholding biblical truth.
Leadership Development:Training local leaders to sustain the mission.

ACTIONS STEPS FOR MOUNTAIN HOUSE OF GOD

CREATE A MISSIONS DEPARTMENTS

Assign a team specifically for international evangelism, including strategies planners, logistics experts and co-ordinators.

IDENTIFY TARGET REGIONS

Use data from organizations like Joshua Project to prioritize unreached people groups and regions.

DEVELOP TRAINING PROGRAMS

Equip missionaries and lay pastors with cross-cultural communication, language skills and discipleship method.

ORGANIZE MISSION TRIPS

Conduct short-term and long-term mission trips to build relarionships, plant churches and train local leaders.

BUILD A GLOBAL PRAYER NETWORK

Unite believers worldwide in prayer for specific regions and outreach efforts.

SECURE FUNDING

Dvelop donor partnerships, grant applications and sponsorship programs to sustain international missions.

MONITOR AND EVALUATE

Set measurable goals for outreach, conversions and church planting.
Regular assess impact and adjust strategies as needed.
